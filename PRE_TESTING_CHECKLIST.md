Taxi Booking Platform - Project Proposal
Prepared For: Awais
Prepared By: Naveez Munir
Date: November 16, 2025
Valid Until: 7 days from date
1. Executive Summary
I propose to develop a professional taxi booking platform that enables your customers to
instantly quote, book, and pay for rides online. The platform will streamline your operations with
an automated booking system, secure payment processing, and a comprehensive admin
dashboard to manage all bookings.
What You'll Get: A fully functional, professional website where customers can book rides 24/7,
with automated confirmations and a backend system for you to manage all operations.
Timeline: 6.5 weeks (45 days) from project kickoff
Cost: £500 (see Section 5 for details)

2.1 Customer Website
Online Booking System
● Smart quote form where customers enter pickup and drop off locations
● Instant price calculation showing exact fare before booking
● Date and time selection for advance bookings
● Passenger and luggage count options
● Multiple vehicle types to choose from (Sedan, SUV, Van, etc.)
● Geofence vehicle selection base on radius
● Refund as per policy
● Real time address suggestions powered by Google Maps
Secure Booking Process
● Simple form to collect passenger details (name, email, phone)
● Option to add flight numbers for airport pickups
● Clear booking summary before payment
● Secure payment processing with stripe
● Option for customers to pay cash to driver
Professional Website
● Modern, mobile friendly design
● "How It Works" section explaining the booking process
● Showcase of available vehicles with photos and features
● Service areas covered across the UK
● Customer testimonials section

Customer Accounts (Optional Login)

● Manage profile information
Automated Communications
● Instant email confirmation after booking
● Booking reference number for tracking

● Professional email templates with your branding

Business Analytics

Configuration & Settings

Reporting & Export
● Export booking data to Excel/CSV
